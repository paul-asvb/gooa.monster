3. Getting Started

To begin, sign up with your email or social media account. Once logged in, you'll be guided through a quick setup process to link your accounts and personalize your dashboard.

4. Connecting Social Media Accounts

Go to the Accounts tab and select "Connect Account". Follow the prompts to authorize each platform. Make sure you're logged into the correct account in your browser during this process.

5. Creating a Post

Click the "New Post" button. You can add text, upload images or videos, select which platforms to post to, and preview how it will look before publishing.

6. Scheduling Posts

After creating your post, click "Schedule" instead of "Post Now". Choose your preferred date and time. The app will automatically publish your content at that time.

7. Viewing Analytics

The Analytics tab shows engagement metrics such as likes, shares, comments, and impressions across platforms. You can view trends over time and export reports.

8. Notifications & Alerts

Stay updated with real-time alerts on post performance, failed posts, or important milestones. You can customize what you get notified about in Settings.

9. Settings & Preferences

Manage your profile, time zone, connected accounts, and notification preferences in the Settings section. Here, you can also enable two-factor authentication for security.

10. FAQ

Q: Can I post to multiple platforms at once?
A: Yes, simply select all the platforms you want to post to when creating a post.

Q: What media formats are supported?
A: JPG, PNG, MP4, and GIFs are supported, depending on the platform.
